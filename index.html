Welcome To Sip And Savor Cafe

Sip and Savor cafe is a place for people who are passionate about coffee - where good runs deep.

At Sip and Savor Cafe is an Independent coffee company conceptualized by people wanting to crate a unique and exciting experience in coffee. For Sip and Savor cafe this means sticking to our original Ideas and not deviating from these. Our continuously changing menu highlights the incredible flavor of the different coffees we serve.

The foundations of our business model are driven by ethics, excellence, and research. We buy from local traders and make our buying decisions based on what is the very best coffee available - regardless of price. We invite you to engage with us about which coffee you might prefer!

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Liam Brown

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.
